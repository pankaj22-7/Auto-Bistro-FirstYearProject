Altroz Specs, Features and Price

The Tata Altroz has 1 Diesel Engine, 1 Petrol Engine and 2 CNG Engine on offer. The Diesel engine is 1497 cc, the Petrol engine is 1199 cc while the CNG engine is 1199 cc and 1198 cc . It is available with Manual & Automatic transmission.Depending upon the variant and fuel type the Altroz has a mileage of 18.05 kmpl to 26.2 km/kg & Ground clearance of Altroz is 165.
