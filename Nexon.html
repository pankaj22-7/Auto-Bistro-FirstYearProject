Nexon Specs, Features and Price

The Tata Nexon has 1 Diesel Engine and 1 Petrol Engine on offer. The Diesel engine is 1497 cc while the Petrol engine is 1199 cc . It is available with Manual & Automatic transmission.Depending upon the variant and fuel type the Nexon has a mileage of 17.01 to 24.08 kmpl & Ground clearance of Nexon is 208. The Nexon is a 5 seater 4 cylinder car and has length of 3995, width of 1804 and a wheelbase of 2498.
